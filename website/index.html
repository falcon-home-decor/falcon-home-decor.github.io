Who We Are

Designing Homes With Heart & Precision

Founded in Dindigul, Falcon Home Decor was born from a simple belief — that beautiful spaces change lives. Our team of passionate designers blends contemporary aesthetics with the rich textures and warmth of South Indian living.

From your first consultation to the final reveal, we walk every step with you, ensuring each choice reflects your personality and elevates your everyday life.
